What Will I Eat?

Food can often be the most expensive part of a trip if your group eats out every day. But there are lots of other options! Buying groceries at a nearby grocery store and keeping ice in a cooler can make a mobile pantry and refrigerator wherever you go!

Here's a great place to start for recipes: 16 Ridiculously Easy Camping Recipes

Don't forget to buy groceries from a local shop to save costs and create nourishing meals!
I recommend the basics:

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Fruit and veggies
  • Hot dogs
  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Ready-to-eat meals
