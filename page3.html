What If I Need Help?

Here's some resources: If you need any help RESOURCES KOA tent camping: https://koa.com/blog/10-tips-for-tent-camping/ REI camping for beginners: https://www.rei.com/learn/expert-advice/camping-for-beginners.html covers essential gear, what to wear, and tolietries. Camping checklist: https://www.americasstateparks.org/camping-gear-checklist/ National Parks Maps: https://www.nps.gov/planyourvisit/maps.htm If camping in national parks, here's a great tool to access maps to over 400 parks! Accessibility: https://www.nps.gov/subjects/accessibility/index.htm This National Parks website can help you plan your visit, learn about accessible features in each park, and even offer trip ideas based on your group's abilities. Safety in National Parks: https://www.nationalgeographic.com/adventure/article/tips-for-staying-safe-in-national-parks
